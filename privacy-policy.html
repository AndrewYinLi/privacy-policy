Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Race Poke collects your mobile phone number solely to deliver SMS updates that you explicitly request, including race registration reminders and related race updates based on the alerts you choose to follow.

Information We Collect

We may collect your mobile phone number and any alert preferences you submit through the Race Poke signup form.

How We Use Your Information

We use your mobile number only to send you the SMS messages you requested, including recurring automated text messages about race registration reminders and race-related updates.

SMS Consent and Mobile Data Use

SMS consent is collected only through the website signup form. We do not collect SMS consent through verbal consent, paper forms, QR codes, or third-party lead lists.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS consent information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share your information only with service providers that help us operate the messaging service, such as our SMS platform provider, or when required by law.

Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on the races, deadlines, and alerts you choose to follow. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out and Help

You can opt out of Race Poke SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact support@racepoke.com.

Data Protection

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@racepoke.com.
