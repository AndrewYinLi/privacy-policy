Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Race Poke collects your mobile phone number solely to deliver SMS updates that you explicitly request, including Abbott World Marathon Majors registration reminders and related updates.

Information We Collect

We may collect your mobile phone number and information you provide through the Race Poke signup form.

How We Use Your Information

We use your mobile number only to send SMS messages you requested, including recurring automated text messages about Abbott World Marathon Majors registration reminders and related updates.

SMS Consent and Mobile Data Use

SMS consent is optional and is collected only if you check the SMS consent box on the website signup form. You may use Race Poke without consenting to receive SMS messages.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS consent information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share your information only with service providers that help us operate the messaging service, such as our SMS platform provider, or when required by law.

Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on Abbott World Marathon Majors registration deadlines and updates. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out and Help

You can opt out of Race Poke SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact support@racepoke.com.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@racepoke.com.
