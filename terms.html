SMS Program Description

Race Poke provides recurring automated text messages about Abbott World Marathon Majors registration reminders and related updates.

User Consent

SMS consent is optional. You may use Race Poke without consenting to receive SMS messages. If you choose to receive SMS messages, you must check the SMS consent box on the Race Poke signup form.

Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on Abbott World Marathon Majors registration deadlines and updates. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out and Help

You can cancel the SMS service at any time by replying STOP to any Race Poke message. After you send STOP, you may receive one final message confirming that you have been unsubscribed.

For help, reply HELP or contact support@racepoke.com.
